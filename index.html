Je suis un scientifique spécialisé en informatique, basé en Silicon Valley où je travaille chez Xilinx. I am a computer scientist based in Silicon Valley where I work for Xilinx. I spent quite some time in research as assistant professor in France where I was also involved in the creation of 3 start-ups, such as SILKAN which developed high-end simulators of whole systems with heterogeneous computing, parallel programming tools and other funny stuff such as the automatic parallelizer Par4All for various platforms such as weird low power embedded systems or GPU. Right now I am pretty excited by SYCL and modern C++ as a high-level way to address heterogeneous computing efficiently.
